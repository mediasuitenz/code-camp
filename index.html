Hamish Watson

What Database Developers need to know about Continuous Delivery

2:00PM - 2:55PM | SQL / Business Intelligence

Hamish is a Systems Management Specialist with a passion for efficient application deployment using DevOps methodologies. He has 18 years IT experience in managing large scale databases on JADE & SQL technologies.

He has been managing SQL Server since SQL 2000 and pragmatic architectural design is his main focus at Jade Software.

Educating and helping others learn is a driver for Hamish and he is a PASS Chapter Leader, confident speaker and a guest lecturer at a local university.

Continuous Integration (CI) is integral for agile development. Continuous Delivery (CD) is an overarching approach to ensuring that applicaiton deployments are reliable, repeatable and automated. It takes the goodness of CI and provides both DEV and OPs peace of mind when it comes to deploying to PROD. Hamish will take the audience on a ride of discovery around what CD can do for developers - especially when it comes to updating the database with new code/structures/data. There will be live DEMOs of CD in action.
